Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is beloved by many for its comforting layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. To make a delicious lasagna, you will need to start by gathering all the necessary ingredients, which typically include lasagna noodles, ground beef or sausage, marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and onion.
